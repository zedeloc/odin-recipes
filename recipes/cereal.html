Cereal

Milk + Grain-Based Pellets = Cereal

Ingrediants:

  • Milk
  • Human Dog Food Grain Pellets
  • Spoon and Bowl

Steps:

  1. Grab your bowl and place upright on a level surface (a table or floor will do).
  2. Pour your Human Dog Food Grain Pellets into the bowl.
  3. Pour milk over Human Dog Food Grain Pellets and say "When" when you have enough milky.
  4. Place spoon in bowl along with contents and enjoy
